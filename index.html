How the City of Durango is Performing

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) show how the City is doing on the things that matter most. They measure progress toward our strategic goals and help ensure accountability to the community. They transform complex data into clear, measurable results so residents and leaders understand what's working and where improvements are needed. By tracking KPIs, the City uses data to make informed decisions, prioritize resources, and improve services.

KPIs show how strategic goals translate into real-world outcomes for the Durango community, allowing us to celebrate successes, identify challenges early, and stay focused on long-term priorities like sustainability, infrastructure, and community well-being.

Ultimately, KPIs help answer the question: Are we moving in the right direction?
