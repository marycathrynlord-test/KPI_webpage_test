How the City of Durango is Performing

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) show how the City is doing on the things that matter most.
They measure progress toward our strategic goals and help ensure accountability to the community.
Ultimately, KPIs help answer the question: Are we moving in the right direction?

Definitions & Indicators

A quick glossary of the terms and colors used throughout — the color of each icon below carries through everywhere on the page.

System Definitions

How the City's work is organized, from policy down to daily operations.

Strategic Plan
High-level community outcomes and priorities.
Key Work System
Major operational systems that create City value.
Key Services
Core services delivered to residents and stakeholders.
Programs
Operational activities and service delivery functions.

Progress Key

What each status color means, wherever it appears.

On Track
Performance is at or above target, or within an agreed tolerance (e.g. within 5%).
At Risk
Below target but recoverable — within a moderate gap (e.g. 5–15%) or trending toward target.
Off Track
Significantly below target (e.g. more than 15% off) or declining for 2+ consecutive periods.
No Data
No current measure has been recorded for this item.
⏱ Stale
Not updated in over 45 days — treat the badge as unverified until refreshed.
